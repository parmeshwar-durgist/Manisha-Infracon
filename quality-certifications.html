Laboratory Audits

Strict Material Testing Laboratory Routines

1. Raw Materials Inspection

Aggregates are subjected to mechanical sieve analysis to verify size distributions. Sand is subjected to chemical wash analysis to confirm the silt percentage is under 3% limits to avoid paste weakening.

2. Concrete Slump Testing

A slump cone checks workability of every batch before loading. Slump ranges are kept within 100mm to 150mm limits based on pump heights and placement settings to avoid hydration segregation.

3. Compressive Cube Testing

We cast 150mm reference concrete cubes for every 50m³ of dispatch. Cubes are cured in temperature water tanks, then crushed in calibrated hydraulic presses at 7 and 28 days to confirm grade performance.

Occupational Safety Standards

At Manisha Infracon, safety is an absolute requirement, not a secondary choice. We enforce safety measures from our batching plant to structural delivery site:

  • Site PPE Requirement
    All operators must wear steel-toe boots, safety goggles, hard hats, and reflective vests.
  • Agitator drum clean locking
    Maintenance staff enter truck mixer drums only after complete mechanical lockouts.
  • Continuous site distance alarms
    Mixer trucks have continuous backup sound alerts and distance radar cameras.

Eco Sustainability Practices

We are dedicated to building structural foundations that preserve planetary resources. Our environmental standards include:

  • Water Recycling Systems
    Runoff washing water is filtered through separation basins to recycle sand and return clear water for mixing.
  • Industrial By-Products Usage
    We replace high-carbon Portland cement with fly ash, silica fume, and slag to capture carbon.
  • Eco-Green Mix Design
    Our specialty Green Concrete reduces structural carbon footprint profiles by up to 50% margins.
