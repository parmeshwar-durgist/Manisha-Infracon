How We Build It

Step-By-Step Production System

From material screening to direct structural placement, discover how our computerized systems control the chemical consistency of each load.

Step 01

Raw Materials Sourcing

Quality concrete begins with clean raw materials. We source aggregates that pass sieve analysis, double-wash sand to remove silts, and store binder cements in temperature-regulated towering silos. Additives are tested for reactivity profiles before acceptance.

Step 02

Automated Batching

Ingredients are weighed on computerized pneumatic load scales. Proportions are regulated by automated recipe algorithms that auto-adjust aggregate measurements to compensate for ambient moisture percentages.

Step 03

High-Efficiency Mixing

Batched loads enter high-shear twin-shaft paddle mixers. Mixing blades revolve at calibrated speeds, securing absolute dispersion of admixtures and complete cementitious binding within 90-120 seconds.

Step 04

Lab Quality Testing

Before exiting the plant, slump-cone testing checks viscosity. We cast reference cylinders (cubes) from every dispatch batch to perform compressive fracture tests at 7 and 28 days in hydraulic press systems.

Step 05

Transit Mixer Loading

The qualified mix flows directly into truck mixer drums. Rotation rates are electronically limited during loading to prevent material segregation. Drums maintain continuous hydration agitation.

Step 06

On-Site Placement & Delivery

Truck mixers reach sites guided by GPS scheduling. On-site engineers confirm slump compliance before discharging into modern boom concrete pumps to place material within the hydration window.
