What We Cast

Diverse Concrete Structural Applications

Explore the 10 core engineering sectors that rely on our specialized concrete formulas for their structural parameters.

Residential Buildings

General low-to-mid rise housings requiring structural integrity, standard setting curves, and cracking protection.

Grades: M20, M25, M30
Demand Profile: Slump maintenance, easy finishing.

Villas

Luxury custom properties requiring premium finishes, sound isolation, and high structural lifespan ratios.

Grades: M25, M30, Self-Compacting
Demand Profile: Flawless formwork finish, low shrink.

Apartments

Multi-story residential communities requiring rapid vertical concrete pumping and rapid formwork turnover schedules.

Grades: M30, M35, M40, M50
Demand Profile: High flow, early compressive strength.

Commercial Buildings

Corporate towers, shopping malls, and office complexes with deep core foundations and wide span beams.

Grades: M40 to M80
Demand Profile: Temperature control, low hydration heat.

Industrial Projects

Chemical plants, metal refineries, and power complexes requiring specialized acid and heat resistant formulations.

Grades: M45 to M100, Steel Fiber
Demand Profile: Chemical shield, high loading fatigue resistance.

Roads

Pavement Quality Concrete (PQC) and dry lean bases engineered to support massive vehicle weight without crack formation.

Grades: M30, M40 (PQC)
Demand Profile: Flexural strength, abrasion resistance.

Bridges

Heavy structural piers and prestressed girder sections placed over river courses or active railways.

Grades: M50 to M80, Silica Fume
Demand Profile: High durability index, zero voids.

Flyovers

Elevated arterial structures demanding high early tensile strengths to support segment assembly lifting processes.

Grades: M45 to M60
Demand Profile: Rapid setting speed, self-compacting.

Warehouses

Large scale logistics hubs requiring super flat concrete floor plates (Laser Screed) to optimize forklift travel.

Grades: M30, M35, Fiber Reinforced
Demand Profile: Low shrinkage rate, high load flatness.

Infrastructure Projects

Subways, marine harbors, airport terminals, and storm canals built to withstand 100+ year cycles.

Grades: M50 to M100, Green Concrete
Demand Profile: Sulfate resistance, massive structural life.

Manisha Infracon is a premier supplier of high-performance ready-mix concrete materials. We guarantee structural strength, durability, and logistical excellence for projects of all sizes.
