Our Legacy

Setting the Standard in Concrete Production

Established in 2001, Manisha Infracon has been at the forefront of providing custom engineered ready-mix concrete formulations to major civil engineering infrastructure ventures and residential projects alike.

Our company prides itself on our robust logistics system, strict adherence to ISO guidelines, and a high-performance team of aggregate experts. We employ fully automated concrete batching plants that continuously deliver accurate ratios, minimizing workability losses in transit.

ISO 9001:2015 Certified
Sustainable Green Mixes

Our Vision

To be the most preferred and trusted construction materials partner internationally, setting global standards for technological innovation and sustainability in the ready-mix concrete industry.

Our Mission

To deliver superior ready-mix concrete solutions on schedule, while maintaining the highest grade of materials, protecting environmental ecosystems, and engineering customer-first service.

Core Values

Commitment to uncompromising quality, strict safety protocols on site, customer collaboration, operational transparency, and continuous scientific research of green materials.

Our Capabilities

State-Of-The-Art Automated Infrastructure

Manisha Infracon operates an extensive fleet of modern truck mixers, concrete pumps, and stationary batch plants. Our equipment represents the peak of modern automated engineering.

High Capacity Batching
Our plants produce over 1,200 cubic meters of ready-mix concrete daily.
GPS Fleet Tracking
Transit mixers are monitored in real-time to assure correct agitator speed and dispatch efficiency.
Advanced Concrete Pumping Fleet
Boom pumps ranging from 36m to 56m length to place concrete directly at any height.
