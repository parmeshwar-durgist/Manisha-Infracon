Engineered Mixes

Specialty & High-Performance Catalogue

Filter through our comprehensive range of 15 custom-engineered concrete mixes designed for specific applications, loading capacities, and finishes.

Structural Core

1. High-Grade Concrete

Specifically formulated for load-bearing configurations. Designed from grades M50 to M100, providing incredible compressive load strength.

Features

High durability index, Low permeability, extreme compressive strength.

Benefits

Reduces column sizes, enhances structural lifecycle, resists environmental erosion.

Applications

Skyscrapers, high-rise structural foundations, dam spillways, marine structures.

M50 to M100
Structural Core

2. Slab Concrete for IHB

Customized for Individual House Builders (IHB). Offers a balance of workability, standard set times, and smooth pumpable placement.

Features

Optimum setting times, cohesive matrix, slump retention of 2-3 hours.

Benefits

Easy to finish, reduces water evaporation cracks, ensures honey-comb free slabs.

Applications

Residential roof slabs, beams, columns, staircases, and floor castings.

M20 to M30
Specialty & Flow

3. Self-Compacting Concrete (SCC)

Highly flowable concrete that flows effortlessly under its own weight into complex formwork without needing mechanical vibration.

Features

Excellent flowability (slump-flow >650mm), high segregation resistance.

Benefits

Zero vibration noise on site, cuts labor costs, perfect architectural surfaces.

Applications

Congested rebar elements, slim architectural columns, retaining walls, tunnels.

Self-Leveling
Structural Core

4. Smart Dynamic Concrete

Next-gen concrete exhibiting high early strength and optimized plasticity. Specially engineered to flow while maintaining low water-binder ratios.

Features

Enhanced viscosity modifiers, superplasticizer adjustments, high early hydration.

Benefits

Rapid formwork stripping (24-48 hrs), superior pumping reach, minimal shrinkage.

Applications

Precast components, post-tensioned slabs, rapid infrastructure repair projects.

High Viscosity
Specialty & Flow

5. Green Concrete

Environmentally sustainable concrete replacing ordinary Portland cement with fly ash, GGBS, and silica fume to reduce carbon footprint by up to 50%.

Features

Eco-friendly components, reduced heat of hydration, high chemical resistance.

Benefits

Reduces carbon emissions, contributes to IGBC/LEED rating, resists sulfate attacks.

Applications

Massive structural rafts, green building projects, sewer pipes, dams.

Eco Friendly
Decorative

6. Color Concrete

Concrete mixed with UV-resistant mineral oxide pigments to create beautiful integral color matching any architectural design plan.

Features

UV-stable pigments, weather-resistant, fading resistant pigments.

Benefits

Color goes through the entire slab, eliminates painting costs, low upkeep.

Applications

Plazas, pedestrian pathways, driveways, swimming pools, architectural panels.

Pigmented Mix
Decorative

7. Stamp Concrete

Textured concrete styled to mimic natural materials like brick, slate, stone, tile, or wood. Highly attractive option with structural strength.

Features

Special stamping molds, high plasticity concrete, surface hardener sealers.

Benefits

Cheaper than real stone, doesn't shift or grow weeds like pavers, slip-resistant.

Applications

Hotel driveways, outdoor patios, theme parks, courtyards, pool decks.

Textured Finish
Structural Core

8. Lightweight Concrete

Mixed with lightweight aggregates (pumice, expanded clay) to reduce concrete density by up to 40% while preserving insulation features.

Features

Density range: 800 - 1800 kg/m³, high thermal and acoustic insulation.

Benefits

Reduces dead load on columns, reduces seismic weight of buildings, saves energy.

Applications

Floor screeds, structural roof toppings, partition walls, light blocks.

Low Density Mix
Fiber Reinforced

9. PP Fiber Reinforced Concrete

Concrete integrated with micro polypropylene fibers to block micro-crack propagation and dramatically reduce plastic shrinkage cracking.

Features

Uniformly dispersed micro-fibers, high plastic tensile load absorption.

Benefits

Prevents early age shrinkage cracks, increases impact resistance, reduces bleed.

Applications

Industrial floors, wall plaster, precast panels, pavement slabs.

Micro-Fiber Mix
Fiber Reinforced

10. Steel Fiber Reinforced

Engineered with high tensile steel wire fibers. Provides high structural ductility, fatigue resistance, and load redistribution capacities.

Features

Dramatically improves flexural tensile capacity, post-crack load bearing capacity.

Benefits

Replaces traditional wire mesh steel reinforcement, saves placing labor.

Applications

Heavy industrial floors, tunnel linings, warehouse slabs, airport runways.

Steel Reinforced
Specialty & Flow

11. Temperature Control Concrete

Concrete chilled with liquid nitrogen or crushed ice to limit heat of hydration and prevent thermal cracking during thick structural casting.

Features

Maximum discharge temperature under 25°C, high performance temperature sensors.

Benefits

Eliminates core-to-surface temperature gradient cracks, improves mass concrete life.

Applications

Massive foundation rafts, bridge piers, columns, hydroelectric dams.

Chilled Mix
Fiber Reinforced

12. Glass Fiber Reinforced

Concrete reinforced with high tensile, alkali-resistant glass fibers (AR-glass), ideal for casting thin architectural panels.

Features

Alkali resistant glass, high flexural strength, lightweight structural sectioning.

Benefits

Casts thin panels (10-15mm), rust-proof, allows highly detailed complex shapes.

Applications

Exterior facades, decorative cornices, simulated rocks, sound barriers.

AR-Glass Fiber
Specialty & Flow

13. Porous Concrete

Also known as Pervious Concrete. Engineered with no sand (fine aggregates), leaving connected void channels that allow water to drain straight through.

Features

Void content: 15% - 25%, high permeability rates (>0.34 cm/s).

Benefits

Recharges groundwater, prevents stormwater runoff, controls surface hydroplaning.

Applications

Parking lots, greenhouses, sidewalk pavements, tennis courts.

Pervious Mix
Structural Core

14. Bag Concrete

Pre-mixed, dried sand, gravel, and cement packed in high-strength moisture-proof bags. Just add water on-site. Ideal for remote locations.

Features

Dry batched under factory tolerances, moisture resistant packaging.

Benefits

Eliminates material waste on site, easy storage, perfect for minor repairs.

Applications

Post setting, fence fixing, small sidewalks, remote location masonry joints.

Factory Dry Mix
Fiber Reinforced

15. Polypropylene Fiber Concrete

Integrated with structural macro-synthetic polypropylene fibers to substitute light rebar, providing high residual strength and flexural toughness.

Features

High-dose macro synthetic fibers, corrosion-free reinforcement matrix.

Benefits

Completely rustproof, reduces cover depth requirements, long-lasting surface durability.

Applications

Agricultural floors, marine docks, concrete structures exposed to high chlorides.

Macro-Fiber Mix
